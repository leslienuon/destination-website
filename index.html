Here are the top things to do in the Siem Riep area. All with in bike ride or walking distance.

The Angkor Wat temple is the oldest and Biggest Buddhist temple in the world. Over the centuries it has become a major tourists spot

Art can be seen all around the Angkor Wat temple. From statues to budhists faces carved into the earth.

Culture in cambodia is amazing, you can learn alot about the aesthetic peaceful way of living by interacting or observing the Khmer traditions, from performances to the everyday people.

Night Life is essential for tourists, during the night booths are set up and vendors sell delicious foods and clothing for a cheap price.
